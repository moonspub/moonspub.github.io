Pub Premium

For the low price of only one key per month, you can gain access to Pub Premium. Pub Premium gives you a boatload of funny commands and perks such as a nifty "[Donor]" chat tag, building hats, and a Civilian voice line every time you join!

Getting Pub Premium

All you need to do is donate Mann Co. Supply Crate Keys to this trade link. Only pure trades (keys only) are accepted for Pub Premium.

There are two payment options for Pub Premium.

  • 1 key/mo (~$1.80 USD/mo)
  • 10 key/lifetime (~$16.50 USD single payment)

Why keys? Paying me in keys through a secure trade is safer and easier for me to give people the things they want. Keys are a universal currency and can be easily traded into my local currency, which eliminates the extra fees and paid services if PayPal or Venmo were used.

Your Pub Premium Status is not refundable.

Premium Commands

  • !vip - Opens the VIP Menu - Gives you easy access to every perk you can use.
  • !voice 50-150 - Changes your voice pitch, use the standalone command to reset it.
  • !trail/!trails - Gives you a Colored Trail.
  • !civ - Removes your weapons and Enables A-Posing.
  • !wepcolor red green blue - Changes the Color of your Weapons using an RGB value.
  • !pets - Access SourcePets and have a fake npc follow you.
  • !eyerockets - Toggle Monoculus Rockets.
  • !buildinghats - Turn Building Hats On/Off.
