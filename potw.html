PLAYER OF THE WEEK: LIBADWAITA

STEAM PROFILE

I go by a few online usernames, although you’re free to call me my real name (April) if you’d like. I started playing Moon’s Pub sometime around April of 2025, and it quickly grew to become one of my favourite online communities. I would try to catch games when active (was a bit hard during the pre steam release) and the weekly Shitpost Sunday. I joined the Discord server in June 2025. In January 2026 I became an admin. When the 24/7 servers shut down, I tried to stay as active within the community and participate in the weekly events that were still being held. I am very impressed to see that despite the community being almost entirely cut off except for one day a week for the past 3 months, that we’ve continued to see a very active, passionate, and wonderful base of players.

However, we are seeing a new era for Moon’s Pub. The fog is clearing and we are walking towards a new era of Moon’s Pub with the servers reopening after this very Shitpost Sunday. Shitpost Sunday Six-Seven (67) will most certainly be crowned by triple T himself as the most labubutastic brainrot sunday of them all, and might make up for the LankyBox disappearance.

All jokes aside, I am looking forward to seeing y’all in the 24/7 servers.
